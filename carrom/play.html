Royal Carrom Rules

Active Baselines:

Each active player sits on their designated side of the board and shoots from their own baseline. The game controls will automatically slide the slider and mirror your targeting direction to match your side!

Game Modes:

  • 2 Player / vs AI: Classic 2-player rules. Bottom player shoots Whites, Left player shoots Blacks.
  • 3 Player: Player 1 (Bottom) pockets Whites, Player 2 (Left) pockets Silvers, Player 3 (Top) pockets Blacks.
  • Doubles: 4-player team match. P1 & P3 (Team A - Whites) vs P2 & P4 (Team B - Blacks) taking turns clockwise.

The Queen (Red Coin):

Can be pocketed after you've scored at least one of your own target coins. To secure the Queen, you must pocket another of your coins on the same or immediately following shot (the "cover"). If you fail to cover, the Queen goes back to the center.

Fouls & Penalties:

  • If you pocket the Striker, it's a foul! Your turn passes and one of your pocketed coins returns to the center.
  • Pocketing an opponent's coin gives them the point and passes the turn.
